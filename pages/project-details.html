Project Overview

This stunning 4 BHK luxury villa showcases contemporary architecture with sustainable design principles. Spanning 3,500 sq.ft., the property features smart home automation, premium finishes, and landscaped gardens. The project was completed in 10 months, incorporating energy-efficient systems and modern construction techniques.

The villa includes spacious living areas, modular kitchen, master bedroom with walk-in closet, home theater, gym, and covered parking for 3 vehicles. Special attention was given to natural lighting and ventilation throughout.
